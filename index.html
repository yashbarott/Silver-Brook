Where Fog Meets Precision

Nestled in the heart of the Russian River Valley, Silverbrook Estate is defined by the delicate balance between cool coastal fog and sun-drenched afternoons. These conditions create one of the most coveted environments in the world for Pinot Noir.

At Silverbrook, we focus on small-lot winemaking, allowing each vintage to reflect the nuance of the vineyard and the rhythm of the growing season. Every bottle is produced with intention—never rushed, never overproduced.

Silverbrook Estate 2024 Pinot Noir

Russian River Valley

Extremely limited production. Sourced from premium vineyard sites. Crafted for purity and finesse.

Tasting Notes

Bright ruby in color, the 2024 Silverbrook Estate Pinot Noir opens with aromas of wild strawberry, crushed raspberry, and rose petal, layered with subtle notes of forest floor and baking spice.

On the palate, it is silky and refined, with vibrant acidity and fine tannins. Flavors of cherry, cranberry, and hints of clove lead into a long, elegant finish.

Winemaking

  • Hand-harvested fruit
  • Small-lot fermentation
  • Aged in French oak barrels (8 months)
  • Minimal intervention

Moments Worth Bottling

Silverbrook Estate Pinot Noir is crafted for the moments that matter—shared conversations, long dinners, and quiet evenings that turn into lasting memories.

Whether enjoyed fireside or overlooking vineyard rows at sunset, this wine delivers a sense of place, elegance, and connection.

The Russian River Valley Advantage

Few regions in the world produce Pinot Noir with the elegance and complexity of the Russian River Valley.

Cooling fog from the Pacific Ocean preserves acidity and extends the growing season, allowing grapes to develop layered flavors while maintaining balance and structure.

This natural environment is the foundation of every Silverbrook Estate vintage.
